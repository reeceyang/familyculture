World Map

My parents immigrated from Hubei province in China to America in 2001 to complete their college education. Other Chinese people immigrated from China to look for job oppurtunities. Some of them stayed in America permanently, but others saved up money to take back home. Another reason Chinese people immigrated to America was to escape the political unrest that was happening in their time.
